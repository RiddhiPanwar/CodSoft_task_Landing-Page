It's not just Coffee
It's Starbucks

Starbucks is a global coffeehouse chain renowned for its specialty coffee and teas, operating thousands of stores worldwide. Founded in 1971 in Seattle, it has grown into an iconic brand offering a variety of beverages and food items. Starbucks is committed to ethically sourcing high-quality coffee and promoting sustainability. The company also fosters a sense of community by creating inviting spaces for customers to connect and relax.
